Our Story

In true modern fashion, this story begins with a swipe right. Cody made the first move after coming across Christina's Hinge profile. Who could have guessed how that one decision would better our lives forever? When Christina saw his profile pop up, she could not help but be dazzled by his charm and his handsome pictures. She also swiped right and just like that, Cody "slid into the DMs" with a joke just corny enough to make Christina laugh out loud. From there, they did not go a day without thinking about and talking to one another. Because it was the middle of the pandemic, our first dates consisted of watching movies, cooking dinner, and playing games together all through Facetime. By the time we were able to meet in person, we already loved one another. Cody proposed to Christina on February 26th, 2022 at a beautiful jazz lounge in Dallas. We are excited to finally become Mr and Mrs C. Hill on October 15th in Burleson, Texas.
